Ciphermania - About Us

We were inspired by a recent passion for ciphers and codes when making this website. Our interest in the topic was primarily sparked by an animated show brimming with mystery, secrets uncovered, and, well, ciphers. This enjoyment became the foundation of Ciphermania.

We decided we wanted to share this excitement with others and dove into research for simple ciphers to explain in Ciphermania, for users to better understand with our coding and decoding tools, and so we gathered five of our finding to hopefully spark in others the same wonder and intrigue that was sparked in us. Ciphers are a most interesting form of communication, and this is our respectful preservation and sharing of the topic.
