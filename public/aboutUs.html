Ciphermania - About Us

Hello! Day here, along with Sophia, and we are the pair for this project site! It really isn't much, but I hope this site looks easy to navigate and fun to learn about. We took a lot of time creating this project, so we hope it turns out worth it in the end for you guys.

We were inspired by a recent passion for ciphers and codes in the creation of this website. Our interest in the topic was primarily sparked by an animated show brimming with mystery, secrets (to be uncovered), and, well, a ton of encrypted codes!. This enjoyment became the foundation of Ciphermania, along with the fact that we couldn't really decide on a topic for the project itself back then.
(p.s. the show was Gravity Falls :D)

We decided we wanted to share this excitement with others and dove into research for simple ciphers to explain in Ciphermania, for users to better understand with our coding and decoding tools, and so we gathered five of our finding to hopefully spark in others the same wonder and intrigue that was sparked in us. Ciphers are a most interesting form of communication, and this is our respectful preservation and sharing of the topic.
